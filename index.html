    What I Worked and WorkingOn

    Predicting Patient Outcomes with Graph Representation Learning

    April 2025 - Present

    Recent ICU outcome prediction models focus mainly on physiological time series, overlooking sparse data like diagnoses and medications. We propose LSTM-GNN, a hybrid model that combines LSTMs for temporal feature extraction with Graph Neural Networks to capture relationships between similar patients. Applied to the eICU database, LSTM-GNN outperforms LSTM-only models in predicting length of stay, highlighting the value of incorporating patient similarity via graph-based learning in EHR analysis.
